Translation:

Consacrated to Diana the Queen, for the good health of the Emperor Marcus Aurelius Severus Alexander Pius Felix Augustus raised by [Mae(?)]sianus, member of governor's guard, accomplishing the vow in the year of the consuls Maximus and Aelianus.
