Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: hederae distinguentes 4-5
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: ?, Romania
Date: Second century AD, before constitutio Antoniniana

Bibliography:

  • D. Tudor, MCA 2, 1956, p. 616-617, no. 157 = I. Stoian, Tomitana, p. 207, no. 11 = ISM II 303
  • M. Alexandrescu-Vianu, Dacia, N. S. 21, 1977, p. 154-155

Text

D(is) M(anibus)
Saturn[i]nus
Biti vixit annis
XXXX et Curi-
5tthie filius et
Valeria et Sabi-
na filias eius. Ziles marita suo
marito benemer-
enti titulum
10posuit.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the Manes Gods! Saturninus, the son of Bithus, who lived 40 years and Curitthie, his son, and Valeria and Sabina, his daughters. Ziles, his wife, to her worthy husband set the monument.
