Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: altar / statue base
Material: limestone
Type of Inscription: votive inscription
Execution Technique: carved
Letters: yes 6-7
Provenance: Napoca
Findspot: in the wall of a building from str. Napoca 8, Romania
Date: starting with the reign of Marcus Aurelius or Commodus, due to the status of the city

Bibliography:

  • CIL III 845

Text

I(ovi) o(ptimo) m(aximo)
Sex(tus) Vale-
rius Satur-
ninus
5dec(urio) al(ae)
Sil(ianae) et col(oniae)
v(otum) s(olvit) l(ibens) m(erito).


File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To Iupiter the best and the greatest,Sextus Valerius Saturninus, decurion of the ala Siliana and of the colonia, fullfilled his promise with joy and for merit.
