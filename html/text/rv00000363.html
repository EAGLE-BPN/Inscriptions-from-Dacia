Translation:

Dasius son of Breucus bought a boy slave, known by the name of Apalaustus or any other name, reek by nation, for the price of 600 denarii, from Belicus son of Alexander. M. Vibius Longus guaranteed the transaction with good faith. It is guaranteed that the boy is healthy, didn't show an iinclination towards theft or any other damaging activity, isn't a runaway of a different master; if any of this is different, Dasius son of Breucus requested the same amount of money back and Belicus son of Alexander agreed. Marcus Vibius Longus guranteed in good faith. Bellicus son of Dassius stated that he received the 600 denarii from Dasius son of Breucus. Signed in the canabae of the 13th Gemina legion, on the 16th of May, when Rufinus and Quadratus were consuls.
