Translation:

To Jupiter the Good and the Greatest, for the good health of the Emperor Caesar Pius, the Roman citizens and veterans of the vicus Noviodunum, by the care of Silvius Cassius ... and the book keeper Caius Alexander, at the Ides of June (13th of June), while Orfitus and Rufus were consuls.


Commentary:

The field of the inscription is very damaged, being difficult to be read.
