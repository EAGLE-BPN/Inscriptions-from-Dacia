Text

Placidae
Reginae
eq(uites) v(otum) l(ibentes) p(osuerunt)
per Proculo (sic)
5princ(ipe) et
[G]aio (sic) opt(ione)

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

For the calm Queen, the horsemen willingly accomplished their pledge, through Proculus, the princeps, and Gaius, the optio.


Commentary:

The field of the inscription is badly damaged.
