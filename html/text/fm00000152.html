Translation:

To Jupiter the Good and Greatest and to Juno the Queen, for the good health of the Emperor Aurelius Antoninus, Martius Philo the mayor of the Ulmetum village raised on his own and on the Bessis expenses on the 2nd of the July in the year of the consuls Orfitus and Maximus.
