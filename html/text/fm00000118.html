Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters:
Provenance: Capidava

Date: Second half of the second century, first half of the third century

Text

D(is) M(anibus)
Ant(onio) Floro b(ene)f(iciario)
co(n)s(ularis) mil(itavit) n(is) XXIII
vixit n(is) XL. Ant(onius)
5Florianus op-
tio leg(ionis) I Ital(icae) fra-
ter posuit.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the Manes. In the memory of Antonius Florus, beneficiarius consularis, who served for 23 years and lived for 40 years. The monument was raised by his brother Antonius Florianus, optio of the I Italica legion.
