Text

[— — vix(it) an(nos)] XXIII ∙ m(enses) ∙ XI[d(ies) — parentes fili]o dulcis-[simo fecerunt. ne]mo cont-[ra legem in hoc sepul]crum co-[rpus inferat aut alien]um vasum[ibi ponat; si quis inf]eret vel aḷị-[enare sepulcrum v]oluerit[— — — — — — — —]ste et ∙ R ∙ P[civitatis Tomita]norum[dare debebit — —]. viator, v(ale).

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

... [who] lived 23 years, 11 months,... days, the parents to their beloved child made [this monument]. Nobody, against the law, is to introduce, in this grave, a body, or is to put a foreign vessel. If somebody will [however] introduce [something], or will alienate the grave, he...and he will have to give the R P city of Tomitans...Greetings, passer-by!
