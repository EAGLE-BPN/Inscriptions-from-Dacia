Translation:

To the Good Fortune! For the fortune, victory and the forever lasting of our Lord Emperor Caesar Marcus Antonius Gordianus, Pius Felix Augustus, and Augusta Sabinia Tranquillina [- - -]


Commentary:

The last two lines were erased and re-engraved after 241, the year when Gordianus married Sabinia Tranquillina
