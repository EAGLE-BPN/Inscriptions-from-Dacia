Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Altar
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters: 4-5
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: Anadolchioi, Romania
Date: 235 - 238 the joint reign of Maximinus Thrax and his son, Maximus; or 244-249 the joint reign of the two Philippi

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, Neue Inschriften aus der Dobrudscha, AEM 6, 1882, p. 11, no. 19 = CIL III 7533 = ISM II 141
  • I. I. Russu, Note epigrafice. Seria IV, SCIV 10, 1959, 1, p. 139-140, no. 1
