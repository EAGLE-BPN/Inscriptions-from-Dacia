Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 6.5
Provenance: Drobeta
Findspot: Reused in the Late Roman fort, in the so-called tower of Justinian, Romania
Date: Second century AD

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 19, 1896, p. 217, no. 78
  • CIL III 14216, 11
  • Gr. Florescu, Mon. Fun. , p. 17, no. 9
  • IDR II 47

Text

D(is) M(anibus)
Prim(us) Ael(ius)
Ion(icus) neg(otiator)
vix(it) n(is) L.
5Iulia Pris-
cilla uxor
et Ael(ius) Iulia-
nus fil(ius), co-
hered(es), per Ael(io) Primiti-
10vo (sic!) lib(erto).
