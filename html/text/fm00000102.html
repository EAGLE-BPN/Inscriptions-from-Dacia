Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: yes 7.8
Provenance: Drobeta
Findspot: Reused in the Late Roman fort, in the so-called tower of Justinian, Romania
Date: Probably second century AD

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 19, 1896, p. 217, no. 77
  • CIL III 14216,10
  • Gr. Florescu, Mon. Fun., p. 17, no. 8
  • IDR II 44

Text

D(is) M(anibus)
Valeria
Gemina
vix(it) n(is)
5XVII d(iebus) X.
H(ic) s(ita) e(st).
P(ublius) Ael(ius) Papi-
rianus 7(centurio)
coh(ortis) III Cam(pestris)
10coniugi
b(ene)m(erenti)
posuit.
