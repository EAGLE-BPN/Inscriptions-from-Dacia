Text

[Imp(eratore) Caes(are) divi
Nervae f(ilio) Nerva
Traiano Aug(usto) Ger(manico)
Dac(ico) pont(ifice) max(imo)
5trib(unicia) ] potest(ate) [.]
[i]mp(eratore) V co(n)s(ule) [V] p(atre) p(atriae)
coh(ors) IIII Gallorum
Q(uinto) Fabio Postumino
leg(ato) Aug(usti) pr(o) pr(aetore)
10[ ...]

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

During the reign of the Emperor Caesar, the son of the divine Nerva, Nerva Traianus Augustus, Germanicus, Dacicus, great pontif, holding the powers of the tribune for ... time, emperor for the fifth time, consul for the fifth time, the father of the fatherland, cohors IIII Gallorum (made the road) while Quintus Fabius Postuminus was governor (of Lower Moesia)
