Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Plate
Material: Marble
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 1.7
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: ?, Romania
Date: The second half of the first century AD, due to the nominative case used for the deceased person, the presence of the hic situs est formula and the abbreviation CPF for the name of the legion.

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 14, 1890, p. 26, no. 51 = CIL III 12498 = ISM II 169
  • M. Alexandrescu-Vianu, Dacia, N. S. 21, 1977, p. 151-153

Text

T(itus) Valerius T. f(ilius) Collina (tribu) Germanus Pes-
senunto immaginif(er) (sic!) leg(ionis) VII C(laudiae) p(iae) f(idelis) donis II don(atus)
vixit an(nos) LVII, h(ic) s(itus) e(st). Heredes T(itus) Valerius Iulianus
et L(ucius) Valerius Ge[r]manus et Valeria Germana et Cl(audia) Iu-
5lia con[iunx e]ius n. b(ene) m(erenti) p(osuerunt).
