Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: altar / statue base
Type of Inscription: votive inscription
Execution Technique: carved
Letters: yes
Provenance: Napoca
Findspot: seen in the area of St. Michael's church, Romania
Date: starting with the reign of Marcus Aurelius or Commodus, due to the status of the city

Bibliography:

  • CIL III 862

Text

N(umini) Aug(usti)
M(arcus) Ulp(ius) Caecil(ius)
Bassianus
[et …]Callisth(enes?)
5magg(istri)
Augustalẹs
col(oniae) Nap(ocensis)
d(ono) d(ederunt).

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the divine spirit of the emperor, Caecilius Bassianus(and)… Callisthenes (?), prssidents of the ordre of the Augustales of the colonia Napoca, gave this gift.
