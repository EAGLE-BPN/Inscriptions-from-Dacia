Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: statue base
Type of Inscription: votive inscription
Execution Technique: carved
Letters: yes 3,5
Provenance: Napoca
Findspot: in "Brückenthor", Romania
Date: towards the middle of the 3rd century, due to the writing

Bibliography:

  • CIL III 7662

Text

[D]eo Soli
ịnvicto
pro salute sua
et suorum
5M(arcus) Cocc(eius) Genia-
lis v(ir) e(gregius) proc(urator)
Aug(ustorum) n(ostrorum)
prov(inciae) Dac(iae) Porol(issensis)
v(oto) l(ibens) m(erito) p(osuit).

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the invincible god Silvanus, for his health and that of his, Marcus Cocceius Genialis, Roman knight,procurator for our emperors of Dacia Porolissensis, fullfilled his promise with joy and for merit
