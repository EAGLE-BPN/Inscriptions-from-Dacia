Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI
Trismegistos ID: 180783
Other IDs: HD046117
Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: stela
Type of Inscription: funerary inscription
Execution Technique: carved
Letters: yes
Provenance: Alburnus Maior
Findspot: In the wall of a house, Romania
Date: 2nd century AD

Bibliography:


Text

[D(is) M(anibus)]
Avilliae Pietatis
domo Aeq(uo)
vix(it) annis
5XXXX Dasa
Suttinis
coniug(i) piiss(imae)
f(aciendum) c(uravit).

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the gods Manes of Avilia Pietatis, from Aequum, who lived for 40 years. Dasas son of Suttinius, for his most pious wife, dedicated.
