Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Sandstone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 5-6
Provenance: Troesmis
Findspot: Iglita, Romania
Date: Early second century, due to nominative case and the presence of hic situs est formula

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 6, 1882, p. 45, no. 92
  • CIL III 7506
  • ISM V 194

Text

[Ti]b. Vitales ex
[b.f.] vet(eranus) leg(ionis) V Mac(edonicae)
[sa]cerd(os)q(ue) provin-
[c(iae)] vix(it) n(os) LX viv-
5[o] se fecit, reliq(uiarum)
[c]uragent(e) Tib. Vi-
[t]ale fil(io) et her(ede)
h. s. e
