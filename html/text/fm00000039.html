Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 5-6
Provenance: Histria
Findspot: Casapchioi, Romania
Date: second century, when V Macedonica legion stood at Troesmis

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 11, 1887, p. 62, no. 123
  • CIL III 7524
  • ISM I 336

Text

Braetius Favor(inus)
vet(eranus) leg(ionis) V Mac(edonicae) mil(itavit)
an(nos) XXVI vixit an(nos) LX
Val(erius) Ingenus filius
5et heres patri pien-
tis(i)mo et Fla(via) Fir-
ma co(n)i(u)gi bene-
[merenti posuerunt].
