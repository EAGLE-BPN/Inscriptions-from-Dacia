Text

Eroni
Inv(icto)
Iaehetav
vilicus L(a)e-
5li Marci-
ani c(larissimi) v(iri)
s(ervus) e
visu c(um) sues
pos(uit)

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the Undefeated Hero, Iaehetav, slave and farm-overseer of Laelius Marcianus, highly regarded man, set it together with his fellows after having a dream.
