Translation:

When Laelianus and Pastor were consuls, on the 23rd of October, I Adiutor son of Macarius wrote this for Lucius Ulpius Valerius, who declared he couldn't write. Thus, the latter lends his manpower to Socration son of Socrates, for a period of one year, for 70 denarii. The payments will be made when established. He will have to work hard and forcefully...Sealed at Alburnus Maior....
