Text

I(ovi) O(ptimo) M(aximo)
et Iunoni Reg(inae) [p-]
ro salu(te) Imp(eratoris) Au(gusti)
civit(as) et reg(io) Histr(iae) cura agent(ibus)
5Cl(audio) Amando et Ulp(io)
Hectore et Aelio
[...]ge arch(ontibus) reg(ionis)

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the Jupiter, the Good and the Greatest and to the Juno, the Queen, for the good health of the Emperor Augustus, the city and the Region of Histria, through the care of Claudius Amandus and Ulpius Hector and Aelius [- - -], archontes of the Region.
