Translation:

To Q. Roscius Murena Coelius Pompeius Falco, consul, legatus Augusti pro praetore of Lycia and Pamphylia, legatus Augusti pro praetore of Moesia Inferior, legatus Augusti pro praetore of Britannia - dedicated (this monument) Annaeus Vibianus, in accordance with his brother's will...Annaeus Vibius
