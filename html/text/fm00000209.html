Type of Inscription: Funerary

Findspot: 3 km from Dulgheru, Romania
Date: Second century AD, most probably the second half

Text

Ἄττας Πόσσει
ζῶν ἑαυτῷ καὶ
συνβίῳ Μάμᾳ
Κρήσκεντος
5θυγατρἰ, ζήσα-
ντι ἔτη ξ΄
τὴν στηλεῖδα
ἀνέστησεν
χα[ῖρ]ε

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

Attas the son of Posseis, still being alive, for him and for his wife, Mama the daughter of Crescens, who lived 60 years, set the stele. Farewell!


Commentary:

See also ISM V 78, the same person.
