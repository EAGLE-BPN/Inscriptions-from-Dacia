Text

In honorem domus diuinae
L(ucius) Ophonius Pap(iria) Domitius Priscus
IIuir col(oniae) Dacic(ae) pecunia sua fecit
l(oco) d(ato) d(ecurionum) d(ecreto).


File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

In the honour of the divine house. LuciusOphonius Domitius Priscus, from the Papiria tribe, duumvir of the colonia Dacica, made it(the monument) at his own expence, on the place given by the decurions.
