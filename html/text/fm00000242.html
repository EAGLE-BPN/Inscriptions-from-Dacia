Text

Imp(erator) Caesar divi Nervae f(ilius)
Nerva Traianus Aug(ustus) Ger(manicus) Dac(icus)
pontifex maximus trib(unicia) potest(ate)
X imp(erator) VI p(ater) p(atriae) co(n)s(ul) V fecit
5L(ucio) Fabio Iusto leg(ato) Aug(usti) pr(o) pr(aetore)

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

The Emperor Caesar, the son of the divine Nerva, Nerva Traianus Augustus, Germanicus, Dacicus, great pontif, being emperor for the sixth time, the father of the fatherland, consul for the fifth time, constructed (the fort), while Lucius Fabius Iustus was governor (of Lower Moesia).
