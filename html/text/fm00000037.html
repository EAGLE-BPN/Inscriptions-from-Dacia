Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Altar
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters: 3.5
Provenance: Troesmis
Findspot: in one of the two late Roman forts from Troesmis, in the western one, Romania
Date: 151-154, when Q. Fuficius Cornutus was governor of the Lower Moesia

Bibliography:
