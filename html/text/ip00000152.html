Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Ioan Piso

Description

Object type: Altar
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters:
Provenance:
Findspot: Sfintesti, Romania
Date: Second half of the 2th, first half of the 3rd century - the nomen Aelius

Bibliography:

  • AE 1968, 444 = IDR II 63

Text

Invicto Mit(h)r(ae)
Ael(ius) Marcianus
et Ael(ius) Eternal(is)
ex v(oto) pos(uerunt)
5pro se et
suos.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Commentary:

The altar was probably brought in the area of Sfintesti from the nearby Dacia inferior province.
