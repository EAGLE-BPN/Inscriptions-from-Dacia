Text

Honori
C(aio) Valerio
Longinia-
no flamin(i)
5qu(aestori) aed(ili) IIviro
mu(nicipii) Tr(oesmensium), Iul(ius) He-
rculanus
aed(ilis) op(timo) socro

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the Honos divinity - in the honour of Caius Valerius Longinianus, flamen (priest), quaestor (book-keeper), aedilis, duumvir, of the Troesmis municipium. Iulius Herculanus, aedilis, raised for the highly praised father-in-law.
