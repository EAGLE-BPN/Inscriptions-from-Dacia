[Imp(eratori) Caes(ari) divi Nervae f(ilio)] Ner[vae Tra-
iano Op]t(imo) Aug(usto)
Germ(anico) Dac(ico) Pa[rthic(o) pon(tifici max(imo)]
[trib(unicia) p]ot(estate) XX, imp(eratori) XII, co(n)s(uli) VI p(atri) p(atriae)
5[Tra]ianenses Tropaeenses
[sub Q(uinto) R]oscio Murena Coelio Po[mpe-

Translation:

For the Emperor Caesar, the son of the divine Nerva, Nerva Traianus Optimus Augustus, Germanicus, Dacicus, Parthicus, holding the power of the tribunes for the 20th time, having 12 imperial salutations, being consul for the 6th time, the father of the fatherland, the citizen of Traianum Tropaeum dedicated / set (the statue) while Quintus Roscius Murena Coelius Pompeius Falco was governor of the province
