Text

[D(is)] M(anibus)
V[i]ctorina
vix(it) [a]nnis XVI
posuit Qu[iet]a
5mat[e]r
b(ene) m(erenti) p(onendum) cu-

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the gods Manes, Victorinua lived 16 years. Quieta, her mother, erected the monument for the one who well deserved.
