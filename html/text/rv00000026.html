Text

In honore[m do]m[us diuinae]
et ob m[erita eius [---]]
L(ucius) Opho[ni]us [Pap(iria) D]o[mi]t[ius Priscus]
IIuir c[ol(oniae)] Dac[icae pec(unia) sua
5fecit l(oco) d(ato) d(ecurionum) d(ecreto)].

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

In the honour of the divine house and based on its merits...LuciusOphonius Domitius Priscus, from the Papiria tribe, duumvir of the colonia Dacica, made it(the monument) at his own expence, on the place given by the decurions.
