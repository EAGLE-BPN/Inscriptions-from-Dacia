Translation:

Ulpius Hortensius - lived 20 years, and Ulpius Balimber - lived 18 years. Cocceia Hortensia, daughter of Cocceius Hortensius, centurion of the legion IX Claudia, wife of Ulpius Balimber, princeps of the legion I Italica, for their beloved sons, made [the monument].
