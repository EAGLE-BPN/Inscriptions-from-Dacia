Translation:

Iulius Alexander promised to give back, when asked, the sum of 140 denari, lent to him by Anduenna daughter of Bato, plus an interest of 12% of the sum a day. Both the initial sum and the interest will be returned in good coin. Contract sealed at Deusara, on the 20th of June, when Rusticus for the 2nd time and Aquilianus for the 1st time were consuls.
