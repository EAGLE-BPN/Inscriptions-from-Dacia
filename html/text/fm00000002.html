Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Slab
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters: Between words
Provenance: Ratiaria
Findspot: Roman Town, Bulgaria
Date: 198-209, the joint reign of Septimius Severus and Caracalla

Bibliography:

  • CIL III 8086

Text

Sedato Aug(usto) pro sal(ute)
Impp(eratorum) Severi et Antonin(i)
Aug(ustorum) et genio col(legii
fabr(um) Q(uintus) Ael(ius) Antoninus
5dec(urio) primus bis(elliarius) magist(er)
coll(egii) s(upra) s(cripti) d(onum) d(edit).
