Text

tilianus mil(es) [le]g(ionis) X[III]
Gem(inae) Antoninian[ae]
libr(arius)
co(n)s(ularis) n(ostri) et Ael[ia]
5Paulina et Ulp(ia) Du-
bitata fili(i) paren-

posuerunt.

Translation:

....tilianus, soldier in the 13th Gemina Antoniniana legion, secretary of our governor and Aelia Paulina and Ulpia Dubitata, daughters, dedicated for their very pious parents.
