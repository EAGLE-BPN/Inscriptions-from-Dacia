Translation:

Iulius Alexander lended, in good faith, the sum 0f 60 denarii to Alexander son of Carus. The latter admited that he received the money in cash and that he will return them, with an interest of 1 denarius per day for the first 30 days. Titius Primitus guaranteed in good faith this transaction and the fact that the sum will be returned and the interest paid in the established day. Sealed in Alburnus Maior, on the 20th October, when Rusticus for the 2nd time and Aquilinus were consuls.
