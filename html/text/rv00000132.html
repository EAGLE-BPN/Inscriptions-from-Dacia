Translation:

To Fortuna Augusta, Ulpius Masculinus, veteran former signifer of the legion 5th Macedonica and decurion of the colonia Napoca, for his and his family's health. Dedicated on the kalendas of September(1st September), when Cilo for the 2nd time and Libo were consuls.
