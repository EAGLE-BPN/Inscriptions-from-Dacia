Text

Q(uod) b(onum) f(austum) f(elix)Vicani Petrenses qui [c]ontul[erunt]
causa salutis cor[poris] sui balineu(m) faciundu(m) qu[od opus]
effectu magisterio anni Nymphidi(i) Maximi et Aeli(i) Gem[ini] ques[tori-]
bus vici Ulpio Romano et Cassio Primitivo curantibus operi N[ymphidio]
5Maximo s(upra) s(cripto) et Aelio Iulio.


File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

May it be good, fortunate and prosperous! The villagers from Petra made their contribution to build a public bath for the good health of their bodies. This work was carried on in the year when Nymphidius Maximus and Aelius Geminus were mayors and Ulpius Romanus and Cassius Primitivus were book-keepers, under the supervision of the above mentioned Nymphidius Maximus and of Aelius Iulius.


Commentary:

sui = eorum
