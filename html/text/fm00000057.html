Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: yes 3.56.2
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: In the wall of a modern house, Romania
Date: Probably second century, when the V Macedonica legion occupied the fortress of Troesmis

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 6, 1882, p. 15, no. 26
  • CIL III 7550
  • ISM II 193

Text

D(is) M(anibus)
P(ublius) Valerius Paca-
tus mil(itavit) in leg(ione) V Mac(edonica)
b(ene)f(iciarius) co(n)s(ularis) duplicarius
5[vix(it) ann(is) ... ].
