Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Altar
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters: 4.40-270
Provenance: Novae
Findspot: Roman Legionary Fortress, Bulgaria
Date: Consular paar from 182 AD

Bibliography:

  • CIL III 6223
  • ILB 283

Text

Bono
Eventui
leg(ionis) I Ital(icae)
M. Maesius
5Geminus
Bononia p(rimus) p(ilus)
d(onum) d(edit) Mam(ertino) et Rufo [cos.]

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

BLABLABLABLBALBALB BDALDBALB


Commentary:

Altar of hexagonal shape, erected by one of the primi pili of the I Italica legion. His origin was Bononia, probably modern Bologna.
