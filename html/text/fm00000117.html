Text

D(is) M(anibus)
memoriae
karissimor-
um filiorum
5\T(iti) Fl(avii) Flavia-
ni et T(iti) F(lavii) Mo-
[desti?]
[---]

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the Manes. In the memory of the most beloved sons, Titus Flavius Flavianus and Titus Flavius Modestus? ...
