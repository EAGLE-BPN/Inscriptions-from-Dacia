File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

When Sabinus and Anullinus were consuls, Aurelius Frontonianus dedicated to the immortal gods and goddesses, for the health of our master and in the honour of the nocturni(firemen?) ; he fulfilled his promise with joy and for merit.
