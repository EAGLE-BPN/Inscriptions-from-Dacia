Text

[Dis] Manibus
[?Aul]uporus
[. . . .]nis vixit
[anni]s c(irca) XX
5[. . . .]ASE CVDO
[. . . .]eni c[oiu]x
[vixi]t annis LXXX - the reading proposed by ISM V

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the Manes Gods! Auluporus, the son of ... lived around 20 years, ... , his wife lived 80 years. (If we are about to understand that the inscription was raised only for Auloporus and his wife, than: Auloporus, the son of...who lived 120 years (!) ...wife, who lived 80 years.


Commentary:

Very difficult to understand. There is no space to mention the both parents, if the deceased have had in fact twenty years. One could also read CXX in the l. 4, which is not be surprising, since his wife lived 80!
