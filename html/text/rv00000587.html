Translation:

A. To good fortune! for the fortune and victory and eternal life of the glorified emperors L. Septimius Severus Pertinax and M. Aurelius Antoninus, Parthicii, Medicii, Britanici, and of P. Septimius Geta, too noble Caesar, and of the mother of the camps, Iulia Augusta, and of Plautilla and for Fulvius Plautianus and for the Sacred Senate and for their sacred armies and for our brilliant consular (- governor) Ovinius Tertullus and for the people of the Left Pontus' metropolis - Tomis - the priests...and the patrons grouped around the priest, for the second time [and] archiereus...B. ...those grouped around...and secretary...Hephaistodoros...Dionysios...Aelius Sos...and euposiarch...Dionysios H...C. Diogenianos...Memnon M...and secretary...manos Vitalion...
