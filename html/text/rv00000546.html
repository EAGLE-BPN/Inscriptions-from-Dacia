Translation:

To the emperor Caesar Trajan Hadrian Augustus, son of the divine Trajan Parthicus, nephew of the divine Nerva, pontifex maximus, holding the tribune's power for the 4th time, consul for the 3rd time, the city (according to the Latin text, the state; according to the Greek text, the Council and the People) of the Tomitans, consecrated (this plaque), C. Ummidius Quadratus Severus Sertorius being legatus Augusti pro praetore
