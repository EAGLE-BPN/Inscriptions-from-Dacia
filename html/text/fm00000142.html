Object type: Stele

Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters:
Provenance: Troesmis
Findspot: ?, Romania
Date: First half of the third century AD

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 14, 1891, p. 19, no. 44
  • CIL III 12484

Text

Quisque henc
memoriam le-
xerit sit illo
semper bene
5sit t(i)b(i) semper
b(e)ne! D(is) Manibus
[[. ] []]

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

Whoever this funeral inscription read, may him be well; be well Thou! To the Manes Gods...


Commentary:

quisque = quisquis; henc = hanc; lexerit = legerit; illo = illi; memoria = funerary inscription/monument in the Danubian Latin
