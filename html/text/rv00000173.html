Translation:

Severus and Quintianus consuls. The names of the Asians.Germanus, cheief of the spira(the association), Tattario, Hermes, Dizo, Gaius, Hyius, Zoilus, Valerius, Zoilianus, Crescens, Eptala, Ulpius, Mucianus, Carus, Surus, Tzinto, Longinianus, Ulpianus, Hermescus, Nepos, Agrippinus, Valerianus, Quirinus, Revocatus, ..., Epipodia, mother, Valentina, Augusta, Asclepiodote, Tiberina, Maximina, Tzinta, Iustina, Cornificia, Longina, Cornelia, Vera, Hilara, Graeca, Rufina, Victorina, Paula. Written by Zoilianus.
