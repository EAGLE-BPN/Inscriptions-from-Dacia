Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: plaque
Material: marble
Type of Inscription: building inscription
Execution Technique: carving
Letters: 5,56,5
Provenance: Ulpia Traiana Sarmizegetusa
Findspot: forum vetus, Romania
Date: 183-185, as Commodus bears the epithet Pius, but not Felix

Bibliography:

  • R. Étienne-I. Piso-A. Diaconescu, Les fouilles du forum vetus de Sarmizegetusa. Rapport général, in AMN 39-40, 2002-2003, p. 114, n. 93, p. 116, pl. XXXV, Ep. 10
  • I. Piso, Le vorum vetus de Sarmizegetusa, Bucuresti, 2006, p. 227-229.

Text

[P]ro salute Im[p(eratoris) Caes(aris) M(arci) Aur(elii)]
Commo[di Pii Aug(usti)]
[M(arcus)] Pompon(ius) Seuer[us de]c(urio) c[ol(oniae)]
[pa]tr(onus) coll(egii) fabr(um) [quaes]t(or) e[t]
5[M(arcus) U]rbius Valerian[us patr(onus) coll(egii)]
eiusd(em) aedem fabr[um pecunia sua]
fecerunt consult[o collegii ?].

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

For the well-being of the emperor Ceasar Marcus Aurelius Commodus Pius Augustus, Marcus Pomponius Severus, decurion of the colonia, patron of the association of craftsmen, questor, and Marcus Urbius Valerianus, patron of the same association, built a residence for the craftsmen's association, on their own expence and with the association's decision (?) .
