Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Fragmentary stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 4.5
Provenance: Troesmis
Findspot: in one of the two late Roman forts from Troesmis, in the eastern one, Romania
Date: Second century AD

Bibliography:

  • CIL III 6202

Text

[[. ] []]
us T(iti) f(ilius)
Vel(ina tribu) Clau-
dianus, Planina, vixit ann-
5um I, me(n)si-
bus IIII di-
em I [[. ] []]
[[. ] []]


File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

[T(itus) - - -]us, the son of Titus, from the Velian tribe, Claudianus, from Planina (Italy, Picenum, Regio V), lived one year, four months and one day [- - -]
