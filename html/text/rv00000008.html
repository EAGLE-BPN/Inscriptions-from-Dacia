Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: plaque
Material: marble
Type of Inscription: honorific inscription
Execution Technique: carving
Letters:
Provenance: Ulpia Traiana Sarmizegetusa
Findspot: forum vetus, Romania
Date: 172

Bibliography:

  • I. Piso, Le vorum vetus de Sarmizegetusa, Bucuresti, 2006, p. 224-226

Text

Diuo Vero Parth(ico) Max(imo) fratri
Imp(eratoris) Caesaris M(arci) Aureli Antonin(i) Aug(usti)
Armeniac(i) Medic(i) Germ(anici) Parthic(i) Max(imi)
tribunic(iae) potestatis XXVI imp(eratoris) V p(atris) p(atriae)
5co(n)s(ulis) IIIproco(n)s(ulis)
colonia Ulpia Traian(a) Aug(usta) Dac(ica)
Sarmizegetusa.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the divine Verus, brother of the emperor Caesar Marcus Aurelius Antoninus Augustus, Armenicus, Medicus, Germanicus, Parthicus Maximus, holding the tribun's power for the 26th time, proclaimed imperator for the 5th time, father of the empire, consul for the 3rd time, proconsul, the colonia Ulpia Traiana Augusta Dacica Sarmizegetusa
