Commentary:

It seems that it is no room for the reading ev(ocatus) on the stone. The presumed V should have been smaller than the other letters and there are not so much evocati legionis attested after Hadrian's reign. There are no clear indication that the inscription should date from the second half of the first century AD and, therefore, a better solution is to see here the sign for centurion, or to read v(eterano).
