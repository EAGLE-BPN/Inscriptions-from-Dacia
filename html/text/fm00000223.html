Text

D(is) M(anibus)
Apolloni-
us Dadae Ma-
mae Dadae
5matri suae
sacerdoti
To[mit]ano-
[rum be]neme-
[renti p(osuit)]

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the Manes Gods! Apollonius, the son of Dada, set to his well-deserving mother, Mama, the daughter of Dada (?), the priestess of the Tomitans.
