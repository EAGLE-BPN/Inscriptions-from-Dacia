Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Base or altar
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters: 2.5
Provenance: Troesmis
Findspot: In the so-called western fort from Troesmis, Romania
Date: After 177-181, when the municipium Trpesmense was founded, but probably not after the middle of the third century

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 14, 1891, p. 19, no. 43
  • CIL III 12481 (6199)
  • ISM V 149

Text

[---]C[---]
.o dec(urioni), q(uaestori) [mu-]
n(icipii) Troesm(ensium)
Iul(ius) Saturni-
55 nus, q(uaestor) muni-
cipi(i) eiusde-
m, o(ptimo) colleg(a)e
