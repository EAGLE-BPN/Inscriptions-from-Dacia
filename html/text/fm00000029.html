Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Altar or statue base
Material: Sandstone
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters: Between words
Provenance: Sucidava
Findspot: in the Late Roman ruins, Romania
Date: The reign of Commodus

Bibliography:

  • CIL III 8042 = IDR II 188
  • I. Piso, Fasti Daciae II, p. 189-197, no. 94=126

Text

[Imp(eratori) Caes(ari)] M(arco) A[ur(elio)]
Commodo Ant[o-]
nino Aug(usto) sub c[u-]
ra Cl(audii) Xenophon-
5tis proc(uratoris) Aug(usti)
Zoticus er Sal[vi-]
anus ser(vi) vi[l(ici)]
posue[runt].
