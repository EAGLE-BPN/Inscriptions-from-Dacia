Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: No 4.5
Provenance: Cius
Findspot: In the Turkish graveyard, Romania
Date: Antoninus Pius, being a veteran that received the citizenship during the reign of Trajan, but who had lived 83 years. Being recruited in his early twenties, he had served 28 years. It had around 45-50 years when he was released from duty. Thereafter he lived around 30-35 years and he died as early as 140' AD

Bibliography:

  • CIL III 12480
  • ISM V 118

Text

Dis Man(ibus)
M(arco) Ulp(io) Domi-
tio Anthio
vet(erano) coh(ortis) Lusit(anorum)
5ex p(edite ?) mil(itavit) annis
XXVIII vix[it]
an(nis) LXXX[II]I
Iulia coniux
po[sui]t [m]ar-
10[ito benemer(enti].

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the Manes Gods! For Marcus Ulpius Domitius Anthius, veteran of the Lusitani cohort, former foot-soldier, who served 28 years and lived 83 years
