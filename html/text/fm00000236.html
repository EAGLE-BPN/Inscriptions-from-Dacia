Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Altar
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Votive
Letters: No
Provenance: Halmyris
Findspot: Reused in the Late Roman fort, north gate, Romania
Date: Probably second half of the second century AD

Bibliography:

  • AE 1988, 987
  • M. Zahariade, C. Alexandrescu, Greek and Latin Inscriptions from Halmyris, 2011, 6

Text

[I(ovi) O(ptimo) M(aximo)
c(ives) R(omani) c(onsistentes) vic(o)]
classicor[um]
cura agentem (sic!) Paparione St[r-]
5atonis magistr(o)
T(itus?) Collumela (sic!) d[e]
suo possuit (sic!)

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the Jupiter the Good and the Greatest! The Roman citizens from the village of the sailors, by the care of Papario, the son of Strato, the mayor. Titus (?) Columella set by his own means.


Commentary:

The stone was cut to be reused in the wall of the one of the towers of the northern gate.
