Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Marble
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters: 1.21.5
Provenance: Histria
Findspot: In the Late Roman building, in the so-called Domus area of the city, Romania
Date: Probably the second half of the second century AD, the joint reign of Marcus Aurelius and Lucius Verus, or the Marcus Aurelius and Commodus, although Septimius Severus and Caracalla can not be at all ruled out.

Bibliography:

  • I. Stoian, Dacia, N. S. 14, 1970, p. 397-401
  • D. M. Pippidi, Studii clasice 14, 1972, p. 201-204
  • ISM I 167
