Translation:

Titus Iulius, the son of Titus, from the Fabia tribe, Saturninus, prefect of the imperial supply service, military tribune of the first Italic legion, prefect of the first mounted Claudia cohort, secretary of the tribune of the plebs, apparitor of the Emperor Titus Aelius Antoninus Augustus Pius, the father of the fatherland, contractor of the custom of the two parts of Illyricum and of the Ripa Thraciae.
