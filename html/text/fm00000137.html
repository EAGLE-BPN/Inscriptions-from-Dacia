Text

D(is) M(anibus)
Have domina Ma-
musa vixit n(is) XXX
C(aius) Veturius Tert-
5ius benemeritae
coniugi Iuliae V-
eneriae fecit sibi
que vivo (sic!) et Vetu-
riae Furniae ma-
10tri carissimae
pientissimae
posuit.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the Manes Gods! Hail you Mamusa, domina, who lived 30 years. Caius Veturius Tertius made and raised the monument for his well-deserving wife, Iulia Veneria, for himself, while his was alive, and for Veturia Furnia his beloved and most pious mother.
