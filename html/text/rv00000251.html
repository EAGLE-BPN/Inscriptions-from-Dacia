Text

D(is) M(anibus)
Aelia Secundi-
na vix(it) an(nis) XIIII
et Aelia Crispina
5vix(it) an(nis) IIII
Aeli(i) Secundus
et Deccia paren-
tes fil(iis) pientiss(imis)
et Ael(ius) Consta(n)s
10mar(itus)

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the gods Manes, Aelia Secundina lived 14 years and Aelia Crispina lived 4 years. Aelius Secundus and(Aelia) Deccia dedicated to the very pious daughters, as well as Aelius Constans, husband.
