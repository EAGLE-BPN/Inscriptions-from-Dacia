Translation:

Maximus son of Bato bought from Dasius son of Verzo the Pirust from Kavieretium a child slave named Passia, aged around 6, who was found abandoned as a baby (sportellaria), for the price of 250 denarii. It is waranteed that the child is healthy, is not a thief, does no damages and is not a runaway. If anyone else whould want the child, he would have to pay to Maximus son of Bato the same amount that he is going to pay to Dasius son of Verzo the Pirust from Kavieretium. Dasius son of Verzo testifies that he received 250 denarii from the buyer. Sealed in Kartum, on the 17th of March, when Titus Aelius Caesar Antoninus Pius for the 2nd time and Bruttius Praesens for the 2nd time, were consuls.
