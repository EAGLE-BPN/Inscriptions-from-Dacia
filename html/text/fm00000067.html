Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Marble
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters:
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: ?, Romania
Date: The first half of the first century, due to its stylistic features - M. Alexandrescu-Vianu.

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, Fouilles et recherches, p. 222, no. 54 = ISM II 187
  • M. Alexandrescu-Vianus, Dacia, NS 21, 1977, p. 153.

Text

Κάλλιστος Μίδα ἐτῶν λε΄ χαῖρε
τῷ ἰδίῳ ἀνδρὶ Μαρκία Μάρκου μετὰ τῆς
θυγατρὸς Καλλίστης εὐνοίας χάριν
ἀνέθηκεν τῳ ανδρὶ καὶ ἡ θυγάτηρ τῳ πατρί.
