Text

T(itus) Valerius T(iti) ∙ f(ilius) ∙ Collina, Germanus ∙ Pes- senunto im{m}aginif(er) {imaginifer} leg(ionis) VII C(laudiae) P(iae) F(idelis) donis II don(atus) vixit an(nos) LVII h(ic) s(itus) e(st). heredes T(itus) Valerius Iulianus et L(ucius) Valerius ∙ Ge<r>manus et Valeria Germana et Cl(audia) Iu-lia con[iunx e]ius n(obilissima?) b(ene) m(erenti) p(osuerunt).

Translation:

T. Valerius Germanus, son of Titus, from the Collina tribe, native of Pessinus, stadard-bearer of the legion VII Claudia Pia Fidelis, receiving twice (military) awards; lived 57 years (and) lies here. His heirs - T. Valerius Iulianus, L. Valerius Germanus and Valeria Germana [sons], together with Claudia Iulia, his too noble wife, set him, deservedly [this monument].
