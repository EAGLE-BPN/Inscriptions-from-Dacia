Text

Imp(eratori) ∙ Caes(ari)divi Traiani Parthici f(ilio), diviNervae nepoti, Traiano  Hadriano Aug(usto), pontif(ici)max(imo) ∙ trib(unicia) pot(estate) XIII, co(n)s(uli) III, p(atri) p(atriae)[senat(us) populusq]ue Tomi[t]anorum.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the emperor Caesar Augustus Trajan Hadrian, son of the divine Trajan Parthicus, nephew of the divine Nerva, pontifex maximus, holding the tribune's power for the 13th time, consul for the 3rd time, father of the Empire, the Senate and the People of Tomis (elevated this monument for him)
