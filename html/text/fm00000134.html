Text

Dis Manibus
Cornelius Pisi-
o sibi et Batsi-
ni coniugi
5sue (sic!) posuit ti-
tulum vibus (sic!)
vixit annis (sic!)
item coniux
annis (sic!), avete!

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the Manes Gods! Cornelius Pisio for himself and for his wife Batsinis, raised the inscription, being alive. He lived ...years and his wife ...years. Farewell!
