Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: altar / statue base
Material: limestone
Type of Inscription: votive inscription
Execution Technique: carved
Letters: yes 2,54,5
Provenance: Napoca
Findspot: str. Măloasă, near the bank of the river Somes, Romania
Date: starting with the reign of Marcus Aurelius or Commodus, due to the status of the city

Bibliography:

  • AE 1966, 310
  • ILD 547

Text

Libero pa-
tri deo
P(ublius) Ael(ius) Mar-
cianus dec(urio)
5col(oniae) Napoc(ensis)
pro(salute) sua et
suorum
v(otum)
s(olvit) l(ibens) m(erito).


File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the god Liber Pater, PubliusAelius Marcianus, decurion of the colonia Napoca, for his heallth and that of his, fullfilled his promise with joy and for merit
