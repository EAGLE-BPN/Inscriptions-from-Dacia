Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Marble
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 1.2
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: In the modern harbour, Romania
Date: The second half of the first century AD, due to the presence of the hic situs est formula and the missing of the DM.

Bibliography:

  • D. Tudor, MCA 2, 1956, p. 581, no. 49
  • AE 1957, 191
  • ISM II 192

Text

M(arco) Sufena M(arci) f(ilio) Pal(atina) Titiano
mil(iti) leg(ionis) V Mac(edonicae) b(ene)f(iciario) co(n)s(ularis) vix(it) an(nis) XXV
mil(itavit) an(nis) VII parentes f(ilio) p(ientissimo) f(ecerunt) h(ic) s(itus) e(st).
