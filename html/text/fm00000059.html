Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Marble
Type of Inscription: Funerary

Findspot: ?, Romania
Date: During the reign of Vespasian

Bibliography:

  • CIL III 14453

Text

T(ito) Flavio Capito-
ni dec(urioni) vet(erano) alae
Pannoniorum
donis donato ab
5[I]mp(eratore) Vespasiano ob vir-
tutem T. Flavius Cas-
tus filius f(aciendum) c(uravit).
