Text

[---]
August[alis col(oniae) Da]c(icae)
Sarm(izegetusae)
aedem ope[re tect]orio et pic-
5turis item sc[alis sigi ?]llis et linteis
exornauit it[em can]delabra ae-
rea duo colleg[io dedit dedicauit]q[ue].

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

...Augustal of the colonia Dacica Sarmizegetusa, ornated the residence with stucco and paintings, as well as statuettes ?, curtains, and he also donated to the association bronze chandeliers and dedicated it all.
