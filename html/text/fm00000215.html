Text

[[. ] []]
[- - -pa-
te]r se vivi [[. ] []]
[.V]al(erius) Valens mile-
5[s l(egionis)] XI Cla(udiae) mil(itavit) ann-
[is] III vix(it) n(is) XXII
[fil]io b(ene) m(erenti) p(osuerunt)

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

While they (his parents) were alive, they raised the monument for their well-deserving son, Valerius Valens, soldier of the XI Claudia legion, who served three years and lived 22 years.
