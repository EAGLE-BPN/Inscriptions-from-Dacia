Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 5-7
Provenance: Drobeta
Findspot: Reused in the Late Roman fort, in the so-called tower of Justinian, Romania
Date: Second century AD

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 19, 1896, p. 215, no. 74
  • CIL III 14216,7
  • Gr. Florescu, Mon. Fun., p. 16, no. 5
  • IDR II 43

Text

D(is) M(anibus)
C(aio) Iul(io) Ve-
recun-
do vet(erano)
5ex dec(urione) al(ae)
Cl(audiae), Cl(audius) Lon-
ginus ami-
cus et he-
res p(ecunia) s(ua).
