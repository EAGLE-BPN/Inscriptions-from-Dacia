[Im]p(erator) Ca[es(ar)] Div[i] Ne[r]v[a]e f(ilius) Nerva Trai[anus Opt(imus) Aug(ustus)]
[Ger]m(anicus) [Dacicus Parthicus p]ontif(ex) max(imus) trib(unicia) pot[est(ate) XX(XXI?) imp(erator) XIII
co(n)s(ul) VII [pater patriae coloniae Ulpiae T]ra[i]ana e Dacic(ae) [Sar]miz[egetusae] forum cum ...... dedit

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

The emperor Caesar, son of the divine Nerva, Traianus Optimus Augustus, Germanicus, Dacicus, Parthicus, pontifex maximus, holding the tribun's power for the 20st(21st?) time, acclaimed imperator for the 13th tine, consul for the 6th time, father of the empire, gave a forum(with...?) to the colonia Ulpia Traiana Dacica Sarmizegetusa.
