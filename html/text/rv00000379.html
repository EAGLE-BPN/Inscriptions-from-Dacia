Text

Iano Gem(ino)
Ael(ius) Baeb(ius) et
Beus(as) Plato(ris?)
v(otum) s(olverunt) l(ibentes) m(erito).

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To Ianus Geminus, Aelius Baebius and Beusas son of Plator, fulfilled their vow with joy and for merit.
