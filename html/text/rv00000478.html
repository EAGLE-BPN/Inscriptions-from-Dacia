Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: altar
Material: limestone
Type of Inscription: honorific
Execution Technique: carved
Letters: yes
Provenance: Histria
Findspot: in the western wall, Romania
Date: 2nd C AD

Bibliography:

  • ISM I 78

Text

ἀγαθῆι τύχηι
ἡ βουλὴ καὶ ὁ δῆμοςτὸν ἀρχιερέα
Γ(άιον)
Ἰούλιον Π̣λ̣λ̣ω̣-
5ν̣α τιμῆς χάριν

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

Good fortune! For the health of Caius Iulius Pollio, priest of the imperial cult, the council and people of the city of Histria erected this monument.
