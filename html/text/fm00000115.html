Text

Dis
Manibus
Coc(ceius) Elius pos(uit)
vivo suo sibi
5et Titi(a)e Matri-
n(a)e co(n)iugi su(a)e
benemerenti qu-
(a)e vixit n(is) XXX
obita ad vil(l)a(m)
10sua(m) titulum po-
suit.


File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the Manes. Cocceius Elius, being alive set the inscription for him and for Titia Matrina, his wife, being worth to be remembered, who lived 30 years and who died at their rural estate.
