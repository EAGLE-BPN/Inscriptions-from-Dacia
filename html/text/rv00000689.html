Translation:

Ampliata, (daughter) of Gennais, from Sidon, built, on her own expense, the altar, for her and for husband Hermes, (son) of Socrates, and for her son, Hermaphilos, (son) of Hermes; for Hermes, who lived 63 years, and for their son Hermaphilos, who lived 25 years; herself, Ampliata, who lived 77 years. Of the elevation of the base, and of the altar and of the stele, until their elevation, took care, from Ampliata's order, Homerichos, (son) of Hermaphilos. Greetings, passer-by!
