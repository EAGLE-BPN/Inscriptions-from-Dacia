Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Marble
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 2.7
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: ?, Romania
Date: Second half of the second century, due to the style of the monument and the shape of the letters

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, Fouilles et recherches, p. 222, no. 5
  • IGRR I 645
  • ISM II 186

Text

Ῥουφεῖνα Ἰάσονος Θεοκρίτῳ Θε-
οκρίτου υἱῷ τὴν στήλην ἀνέστη-
σεν ναυκλήρῳ τῷ καὶ βασιλεῖ
ζήσαντι ἔτη κβ΄ μῆνας θ΄
5χαίρετε.
