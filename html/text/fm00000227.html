Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: No 8.5
Provenance: Histria
Findspot: Reused in the Late Roman fortification, Romania
Date: Second century AD

Bibliography:

  • V.Pârvan, Histria IV, p. 684-685, no. 53
  • V. Pârvan, Dacia 2, 1925, p. 222, no. 23
  • ISM I 282

Text

D(is) I(nferis) M(anibus)
Victor filius Co-
[rneli .. ? ..]IA
[... vixit an-]
5nis XVI, cui sapul-
chrum (sic!) fecit et
titulum posu-
it Cornelius pa-
ter filio pientis-
10simo.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the Manes Gods of the Underworld! Victor the son of Cornelius ... lived 16 years, to whom, the most pious son, his father Cornelius made the grave and set the tombstone.
