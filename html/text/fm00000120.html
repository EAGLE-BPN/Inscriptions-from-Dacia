Translation:

To Jupiter, the Best and the Greatest, and to Juno, the queen, the Roman citizens and the Bessi people, who lived in the Ulmetum village, for the safety of the Emperor Aelius Antoninus Caesar, through the mayor Lucius Valerius Maxellius willingly and fulfilling his pledge, raised the altar by his own expenses, while the Emperor Antoninus was consul for the third time.
