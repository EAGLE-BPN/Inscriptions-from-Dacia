Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Altar
Material: Marble
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 3.5
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: During the construction of a house., Romania
Date: Second half of the second century, after the shape of the letters.

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 17, 1894, p. 31, no. 20
  • ISM II 185

Text

Γάιος καὶ Διογένης
Εἴα μητρὶ γλυκυτά-
τη συνζησάση ἡ-
μῶν τῷ πατρὶ Διο-
5γένῃ ἔτη λδ΄
ἀμέμπτως καὶ εὐ-
σεβῶς μνήμης
χάριν.
