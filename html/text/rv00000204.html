Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: plaque
Material: limestone
Type of Inscription: epitaph
Execution Technique: carved
Letters: yes 4,55,7
Provenance: Napoca
Findspot: the late Roman necropolis, between the streets C.Brâncuși and N. Titulescu, Romania

Bibliography:

  • AE 2000, 1242

Text

D(is) M(anibus).
Tu qui reciprocam se-
mitam voto subis
morare paulum.Vitam
5cognoscis tuam.
Hic sumus expositi mor-
talia munera functi
[---]es
[---].


File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the gods Manes. You, that goes in the opposite direction, wait a moment. You understand your own life. We are buried here, we who fulfilled our mortal dues...
