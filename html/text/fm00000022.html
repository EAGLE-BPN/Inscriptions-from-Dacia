Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: Between words 3-4
Provenance: Troesmis
Findspot: in one of the two late Roman forts from Troesmis, in the eastern one, Romania
Date: Second century AD, probably during the stationing of the V Macedonica in the fortress of Troesmis, before 168 AD

Bibliography:

  • L. Renier, CRAI 1865, p. 299
  • CIL III 6203
  • ISM V 193

Text

[Dis Ma]nibus
[T(itus) Ras]canius
[For]tunatus
[Poll]ia Faventia
5[medic]us an(norum) L h(ic) s(itus) e(st)
[cui m]on(u)mentum
[Rasca]nia Phoebe et
[Rasca]nius Eutychu[s]
[herede]s f(aciendum) c(uraverunt) h(oc) m(onumentum) h(eredem) n(on) s(equetur).

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Commentary:

Nowadays only the left half of the monument is preserved. The other half disappeared shortly after its discovery.
