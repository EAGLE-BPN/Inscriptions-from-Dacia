[Imp(erator) Caes(ar) Di]vi Hadriani fil(ius) [divi]

[Hadrianus A]ntoninus Aug(ustus) [pius]
5[tribunicia] potest(ate) VI co(n)s(ul)Ị[II p(ater) p(atriae)]
[fecit per al]ạm II Pannoni[orum]

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

The emperor Caesar Titus Aelius Hadrianus Antoninus Augustus, the Pious, son of the divine Hadrian, grandson of the divine Trajan, conqueror of the Parths, graet grandson of the divine Nerva, invested with the tribun's power for the 6th time, consul for 3 times, father of the Empire, built by ala II Pannoniorum.
