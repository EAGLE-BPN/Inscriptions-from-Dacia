Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Altar
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Votive
Letters: Yes 4.5
Provenance: Halmyris
Findspot: In the Late Roman fort, reused in the northern gate, Romania
Date: 200 AD

Bibliography:

  • AE 1988, 988 and 990
  • AE 2003, 1550
  • M. Zahariade, C. Alexandrescu, Greek and Latin Inscriptions from Halmyris, 2011, 9.

Text

I(ovi) O(ptimo) M(aximo)
c(ives) R(omani) c(onsistentes)
vic(o) [c]lass(icorum) c(uram)
ag(ente) M[ar]cio mag(istro)
5Victor[ino et]
Sever[o co(n)s(ulibus)].

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To Jupiter the Good and the Greatest! The Roman citizens from the village of the sailors set through the care of Marcius, the mayor, while Victorinus and Severus were consuls.
