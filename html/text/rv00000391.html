Object type: altar

Type of Inscription: votive inscription
Execution Technique: carved

Provenance: Alburnus Maior
Findspot: Èšarina, Romania

Bibliography:


Text

I(ovi) O(ptimo) Max(imo)
Implaius
Linsanti
v(otum) s(olvit) l(ibens) m(erito).

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To Jupiter the best and the greatest, Implaius son of Linsas, fulfilled his vow with joy and for merit.
