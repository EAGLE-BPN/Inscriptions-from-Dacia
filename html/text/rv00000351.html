Translation:

Andueia daughter of Bato bough and received half a house, the right half from the entry door, house that is situated in Alburnus Maior, in the quarter of the Pirusts, having Platorem Acceptianum and Ingenum Callisti as neighbours, as well as other neighbours and the road. The house was bought from Veturius Valens for 300 denari. The half of house, as well as its fences and annexes, as it is without damage, is the property of Andueia daughter of Bato. If anyone would want to take this property away from Andueia daughter of Bato, the price would be of 300 denari, the sum she has promised to Veturius Valens. As well, they agrred that Veturius Valens is to pay the taxes for the house, until the next census. Sealed at Alburnus Maior, on the 6th of May, when Quintillus and Priscus were consuls
