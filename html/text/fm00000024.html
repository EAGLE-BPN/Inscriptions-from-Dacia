Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Altar
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters: 7-8
Provenance: Troesmis
Findspot: in one of the two late Roman forts from Troesmis, in the western one, Romania
Date: 173 AD, the date of the second consulship of Ti. Claudius Pompeianus

Bibliography:

  • CIL III 6176
  • ILS 1108
  • ISM V 146

Text

Tib(erio) Cl(audio) Pom-
peiano c(larissimo) v(iro)
bis consuli
C. Val(erius) Firmus
57(centurio) leg(ionis) I Ital(icae).

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Commentary:

For Ti. Claudius Pompeianus' career, the son-in-law of the emperor Marcus Aurelius see PIR2 C, 973; A. Stein, RE III, 1899, col. 2843-2845.
