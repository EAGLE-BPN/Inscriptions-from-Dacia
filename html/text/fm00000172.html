Translation:

For the good health (?) of our Lords the Emperors Caius Valerius Diocletianus Pius Felix Augustus and Marcus Aurelius Valerius Maximianus Pius Felix Augustus and Flavius Valerius Constantius and Galerius Valerius Maximianus, the noble Caesars, (consacrated to ?) Jupiter and to Fortuna through the care of Aurelius Probus (?)
