Text

Invicto Mit(h)r(ae)
Ael(ius) Marcianus
et Ael(ius) Eternal(is)
ex v(oto) pos(uerunt)
5pro se et
suos.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the undefeated Mithras, Aelius Marcianus and Aelius (A)eternalis made it after their pledge for them and for theirs fellows.
