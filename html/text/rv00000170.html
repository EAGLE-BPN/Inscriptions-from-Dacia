Text

Imp(eratori) Caesari
Marco Anto-
nio Gordiano
pio felici in-
5victo Aug(usto) p[on-]
tif(ici) max(imo)
[trib(unicia)]
p[ot(estate)[---]]
[------]

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the emperor Caesar Marcus Antonius Gordianus, the pious, the happy, the invincible, Augustus, pontifex maximus, holding the tribun's power....
