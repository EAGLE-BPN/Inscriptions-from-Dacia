Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: instrumentum(lead ingot)
Material: lead
Type of Inscription: label
Execution Technique: stamped
Letters: 0,84,2
Provenance: Ulpia Traiana Sarmizegetusa
Findspot: forum vetus, Romania
Date: 106-117

Bibliography:

  • I. Piso, inRömischeStädte und Festungen an der Donau(Akten der regionalen Konferenz Beograd 16-19Oktober 2003), Belgrade, 2005, p. 122-124, fig. 2
  • I. Piso, Le vorum vetus de Sarmizegetusa, Bucuresti, 2006, p. 220-222

Text

1
Imp(eratoris) Tr(aiani) me(talla) Ulp(iana)
2
Imp(eratoris) Tr(aiani) met(alla) [.. ?]
3
C(oloniae) [Ulp(iae) D(acicae) S(armizegetusae)]
4
C(oloniae) Ulp(iae) D(acicae) S(armizegetusae).

a. The ulpian mines of emperor Trajan. b. The...mines of emperor Trajan. c. Coloniei Ulpia Dacica Sarmizegetusa. d. Coloniei Ulpia Dacica Sarmizegetusa
