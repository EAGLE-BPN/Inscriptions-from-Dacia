Translation:

a) To my beloved wife, Euphrosyne, Castresius, Iulius Fronto primilarius' actor, constructed (the tomb); she lived 25 years.b) The actor, still being alive, for him and for his wife, Euphrosyne, who lived 25 years.


Commentary:

Two inscriptions in the tabula ansata on the two sides of the sarcophagus: first inscription (a) on the long side; second inscription (b) on the smaller side
