Text

Tib(erius) Cl(audius) Ianuarius
Aug(ustalis) col(oniae) patr(onus) dec(uriae) I
picturam porticus
et accubitum item Cl(audius) Verus filius eius
5ob honorem dupli
proporticum et culi-
nam et frontalem
ex suo fecerunt.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

TiberiusClaudius Ianuarius, Augustal of the colonia, patron of the 1st decuria,financed the painting of the portico as well as the beds; his son Claudius Verus, with the occasion of double ratio, financed the porticos, a kitchen and a fronton.
