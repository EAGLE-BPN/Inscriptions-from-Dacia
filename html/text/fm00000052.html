Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 4-6
Provenance: Ibida / Aegyssus
Findspot: Slava Rusa / Tulcea, Romania
Date: First half of the second century, when legion V Macedonica is attested in the area

Bibliography:

  • Tocilescu, Fouilles et recherches, p. 208, no. 34 = CIL III 14433 = Em. Doruţiu-Boilă, SCIV 15, 1964, p. 132, no. 6 = ISM V 227
  • Conrad, Grabstelen, no. 235

[... vet(erano) leg(ionis)]
V Mac(edonicae)
et Coccce-
iae (sic!) Dori-
5n(a)e coniu-
gi eius
Antonia
Cr[i]spina
et [A]lex-
