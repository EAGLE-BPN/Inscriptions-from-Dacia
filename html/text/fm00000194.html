Indici
Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: No
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: ?, Romania
Date: Third century AD. S. Conrad dates to the middle of the third century AD. Nevertheless, after the onomastic criteria it should be rather dated in second half of the second century.

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 6, 1882, p. 17, no. 34 = CIL III 7567 = ISM II 182
  • D. Tudor, MCA 2, 1956, p. 610-611, no. 131 (the upper fragment)
  • Both monuments: CCET IV 38 = S. Conrad, Grabstelen, p. 164-165, no. 145 = ISM II, Suppl., 182.

Iulio Puplio f(ilio) Magni

Translation:

To the Manes Gods! For Iulius Puplius, the son of Magnus, ... the father and the mother Iulia Cocceia and the brothers Iulius Antonius and Iulius Antonino
