Translation:

To the gods Manes of Aurelia Marcia, who lived 50 years, Aurelia Provincia who lived 30 years, Aurelius Marcellinus who lived 12 years, Aurelius Marcianus, scribe of the colonia, to the sweetest mother, most beloved wife and son, dedicated.
