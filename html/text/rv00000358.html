Translation:

I Marcus wrote this at the request of Aelius Restitutus, also called Senior, who stated he couldn't write. The latter lent his manpower to work in the goldmines of Titus son of Beusas, also called Bradua, from now until the 15th of November, for the payment of 105 denarii. He has now received 25 denarii and he will received the rest as arranged. He will have to work strongly and seriously. And if he will abandon work without the leaseholder's agreement...
