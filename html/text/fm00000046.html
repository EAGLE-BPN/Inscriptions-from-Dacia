Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters:
Provenance: Novae
Findspot: Steclen, Bulgaria
Date: Probably second half of the first century - due to the use of hic situs est formula together with the name of the deceased person in the nominative case, habit which almost disappeared on the inscriptions from Lower Moesia after Trajan.

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, Monumentele epigrafice si sculpturale, p. 266-268, no. 25
  • CIL III 6232 = ILBulg 311
  • S. Conrad, Grabstelen, p. 234-235, no. 403
