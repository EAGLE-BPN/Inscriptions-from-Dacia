Translation:

Between Cassius Frontinus and Iulius Alexander an partnership is sealed, for lending moeny with interest, from the 19th of January of the year when Pudens and Polio were consuls until the 14th of next April; any gain or loss registered during the given period will be equally divided between the two. Iulius Alexander brought, as cash and interests, 500 denarii and Secundus, slave and administrator of Cassius Palumbus brought 227...Frontinus?sent to Alburnus...? If in this association any of the involved parts will make any fraud, he will have to pay....1 as....1 denarius...20 denarii ?....and after the passing of the term and after subtracting the debts, they will have to get back the initial sums and if there will be an excedent, they will divide it between them. Cassisus Frontinus asked through stipulatio and Iulius Alexander promised by spontio. two identical sets of tablets were signed. As well, Lossa must receive 50 denarii from the associates aforementioned. Sealed at Deusara, on the 28th of March, when Verus for the third time and Quadratus were consuls.
