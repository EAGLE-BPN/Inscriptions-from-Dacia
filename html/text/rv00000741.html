Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI

Description

Object type: fragment of an architrave
Material: limestone
Type of Inscription: honorific
Execution Technique: carved
Letters: no
Provenance: Histria
Findspot: Istria, Romania
Date: 155 or 145 AD

Bibliography:

  • ISM I 152

Text

Imp(eratori) Caes(ari) [divi Hadriani filio, divi Traiani Parthici nepoti, divi Nervae] pronepoti, Tito Aelio Hadriano Antonino Aug(usto) Pio, pontifici maximo, tr(ibuniciae) pot(estatis) [X]VIII co(n)s(uli) I[I]II [p(atri) p(atriae) et M(arco) Aurelio Vero Caesari, co(n)s(uli) II], [civitas Histrianorum, T(ito) Flavio Longino Q(uinto) Marcio Turbone leg(ato) Aug(usti) pr(o) pr(aetore)].

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the emperor Caesar, son of the divine Hadrian, grandson of the divine Trajan Parthicus, great-grandson of the divine Nerva, Titus Aelius Hadrianus Antoninus Augustus Pius, Great Pontiff, holding the tribun's power for the 18th time, consul for the 4th time, father of the empire, and Marcus Aurelius Casarus, consul for the 2nd time, the town of Histia, in the time when Titus Flavius Longinus Quintus Marcius Turbo, was governor with pretorian power governor of Moesia
