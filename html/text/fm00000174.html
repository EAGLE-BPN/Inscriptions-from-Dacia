Bibliography:

Text

I(ovi) O(ptimo) M(aximo)
Doly[c]heno pro
salutem (sic!) Imp(eratoris) D(omini) n(ostri) Pi(i) VS
Felicis Aug(usti) S(e)veri Alexandr(i)
5Luginus Dometi(i) et Aquila
Barsemon er Flavius
Damas sac(erdotes) templum
a solo f(e)cerunt.


File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To Jupiter the Good and the Greatest from Doliche, for the good health of the Emperor, our Lord Pius Felix Augustus Severus Alexander, Luginus, the son of Dometius, Aquila Barsemon and Flavius Damas, the priests constructed the temple from the ground.
