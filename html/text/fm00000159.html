Text

[[. ] []]
pro sal(ute) [Imp(eratoris)---]
T(itus) Fl(avius) Apollinaris
praef(ectus) alae I Dardan(orum)
5qui et do[m]um a sol[o]
[s]umptib(us) suis feci[t]
[ad]ventantibu[s]
[c]ollegis feliciter

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

[- - -] for the good health of the Emperor(?). Titus Flavius Apollinaris, prefect of the first ala of the Dardanians, who also built a house (a place of gathering) on his own expenses for his comrades who were about to arrive well.
