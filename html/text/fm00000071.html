Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Plate
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 3.5
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: Cumpăna, Romania
Date: Probably second century AD, due to the shape of the letters.

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 8, 1884, p. 15, no. 43
  • ISM II 203

Text

[...ζήσασα ἔτη]
ο΄ συνοικήσασα
μοι ἔτη μς΄ ἐξ ἧς
μοι ἐγένοντο
5τέκνα ζῶντα ζ΄
ζῶν φρονῶν κα-
τεσκεύασα τὸ
μνειμεῖον χαῖ-
ρε παροδεῖτα.
