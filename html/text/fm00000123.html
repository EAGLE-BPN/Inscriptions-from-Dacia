Commentary:

The reading of the first two lines is still controversial, due to the numerous ligatures. The reading which took into consideration a vicus Vergobrittiani, a name derived from the Celtic Vercobretus, is probably not correct and therefore there is a "ghost-name", see Matei-Popescu, Falileyev
