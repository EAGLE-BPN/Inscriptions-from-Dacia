Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Altar
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Votive
Letters: hederae distinguentes 3.5
Provenance: Histria
Findspot: In the northern wall of the early Christian basilica, next to the big tower on the western side of the city walls, Romania
Date: 152 AD, after the consuls

Bibliography:

  • I. I. Russu, SCIV 9, 1958, p. 54
  • D. M. Pippidi, Studii de istorie a religiilor antice, Bucharest, 1969, p. 219-223, no. 1
  • ISM I 130

Text

Heroni invic-
to Sicinius
Porphyrio
pro salutem (sic!)
5suam et suorum
ex imperio Hero-
nis lib(ens) a[n]iimo (sic!) feci
Glabrione et Homu-
llo co(n)s(ulibus).
