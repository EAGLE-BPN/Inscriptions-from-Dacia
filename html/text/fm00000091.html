Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Marble
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters: 1.21.5
Provenance: Histria
Findspot: In the southern part of the Late Roman city, Romania
Date: First half of the second century AD, either 129-131, or 159-160, subsequently different identifications of the consular named in the l. 3.

Bibliography:

  • V. Pârvan, Dacia 2, 1925, p. 318, no. 31
  • CIMRM II 2296
  • ISM I 137
