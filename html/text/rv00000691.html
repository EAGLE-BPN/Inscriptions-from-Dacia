Description

Object type: fragment of a plaque
Material: marble
Type of Inscription: funerary
Execution Technique: carved

Date: 2nd/3rd C AD

Bibliography:

  • ISM II 292

Text

Ῥοῦφον Πρίσκου, ἱερατεύ̣σαντα θεῷ Δο-λοχήνῳ ἔτη π(έντε) Ἰουλιανὸς Ἀλεξάνδρ̣-ου τὸν {τὸν} συνιερέα ἐν ἰδίῳ τόπῳ̣      μνήμης χάριν.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To Rufus, (son) of Priscus, who was priest of the God Dolichenus for 5 years, Iulianus, (son) of Alexandros, to his colleague of priesthood [elevated the monument], on his spot, as remembrance.
