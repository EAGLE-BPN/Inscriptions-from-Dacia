Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: sarcophagus
Type of Inscription: epitaph
Execution Technique: carved
Letters: no
Provenance: Napoca
Findspot: unknown, Romania
Date: the 3rd century

Bibliography:

  • Mezerzius,Vat. Lat 5250
  • CIL III 866

Text

D(is) M(anibus)
Aurelia[Ma]r[in]a
vix(it) n(is) XVII Aur(elius)
Marinianus fil(ius)
5eius vix(it) anno I
m(ensibus) IIII
Aur(elius) Babus
vet(eranus) pater p(osuit).
Opt(o) s(it) t(ibi) t(erra) l(evis).


File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the gods Manes of Aurelia Marina, who lived 17 years,to Aurelius Marinianus, her son, who lived 4 months, Aurelius Babus, veteran, her father dedicated. May you rest in peace.
