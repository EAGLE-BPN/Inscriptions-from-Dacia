Translation:

Emperor Caesar M. Aurelius Antoninus, [hailed?] as emperor for the second time, Augustus, pontifex maximus, [empowered] with the tribune's power for the 16th time, consul for the 3rd time, and emperor Caesar L. Aurelius Verus, Armeniacus, Augustus, [empowered] with the tribune's power for the 2nd time, consul for the 2nd time, sons of the divine Pius T. Antoninus, nephews of the divine Hadrian, great-grandsons of the divine Trajan, Parthicus, great-great-grandsons of the divine Nerva. From Callatis (?) thousand steps
