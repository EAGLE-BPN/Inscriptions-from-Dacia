Translation:

To Jupiter the Good and the Greatest, for the good health of the Emperor, our Lord Marcus Iulius Philippus and Marcus Iulius Philippianus, noble Caesar, and Otacilia Severa Augusta, the archontes from the regio of Histria while Aurelius Euxinus, the son of Leontiscus, was the (first) archon, ...the son of Cocieius, Aurelius Victor, Ulpius Martinus, Aelius Iulius, Aurelius Castus, Mucatra the son of ..., Messius Frontinus, ..., Silius Ingenus, Herculius Marcianus, ... raised (this altar).
