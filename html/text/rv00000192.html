Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: monumnet base, reused as sarcophagus
Material: limestone
Type of Inscription: epitaph
Execution Technique: carved
Letters: no
Provenance: Napoca
Findspot: Str. Avram Iancu 30, Romania
Date: the 3rd century

Bibliography:

  • IGLR 436
  • AE 1976, 577
  • AE 1988, 945

Text

D(is) M(anibus)
Cl(audius) Valentinus
vix(it) annis XX
Valeria Valenti-
5na filio piissim(o)
Opt(o) s(it) t(ibi) t(erra) l(evis).

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the gods Manes of Claudius Valentinus, who lived 20 years,ValeriaValentina dedicated to her very pious son. May you rest in peace.
