Text

Coh(ortis) II Br(i)t(annicae) S(everianae).

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

Appartenant à la cohors II Britannica Severiana.


Commentary:

J. Szilágyi: Coh(ortis) II Br(i)t(annicae) S(everianae) sive s(agittariorum); Al. V. Matei, I. Bajusz: Coh(ortis) II Br(i)t(annicae) s(agittariorum); N. Gudea (1983, p. 155) doute aussi de l’épithète S(everianae); C. C. Petolescu (ILD): Coh(ors) II Br(i)t(onum) (milliaria).
