Text

D(is) M(anibus)
C(aio) Iulio Ca-
pitoni ex ses-
q(uiplicario) vet(erano) vixit an
5n(is) LXV milita-
vit n(is) XXV
hic situs est
Iulius Capi-
t[o fil(ius) et h]er(es) e
10[ius b(ene) m(erenti) posuit].


File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the Manes. In the memory of Caius Iulius Capito, who lies here, former sesquiplicarius and veteran, who lived 65 years and served 25 years. The monument was erected by his son and heir, Iulius Capito.
