Description

Object type: Sarcophagus
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters:
Provenance: Noviodunum
Findspot: In the Roman graveyard, next to the tumulus no. 26, Romania
Date: Second century AD, on paleographic grounds

Bibliography:

  • E. Bujor, G. Simion, MCA 7, 1961, p. 396-397
  • BullEp 1971, no. 444
  • ISM V 280

Text

Θ(εοῖς) Κ(αταχθονίος)
Ἀλέξανδρα
Ἀλεξάνδρῳ Ἡρακλέωνος τ-
ῷ πατρινεῖ καὶ γερουσιάστῃ τὴν σορὸν ἔθ-
5ηκε, χέρε

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the gods of the underworld! Alexandra made the sarcophagus for Alexandros, the son of Herakleon, her father-in-law and member of the gerousia. Farewell!


Commentary:

Inscription in a tabula ansata
