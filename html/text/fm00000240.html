Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Sandstone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: Yes 6-8
Provenance: Halmyris
Findspot: In the Late Roman fort, used as threshold in the phase II of the northern gate, Romania
Date: Second half of the second century AD

Bibliography:

  • AE 1991, 1387
  • Conrad, Grabstelen, p. 182-183, no. 216
  • M. Zahariade, C. Alexandresch, Greek and Latin Inscriptions from Halmyris, 2011, 25

Text

D(is) M(anibus)
Memmia Mar-
cia vixit
annis XVIIII
5Mem(mius) Mar-
cus pater
eius et Fl(avius) Mar-
tinus mari-
tus eius et
10Fl(avius) Secun-
dus d(ispensator) eius
coniugi
p(ientissimae) p(osuerunt)

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

To the Gods of the Underworld! To Memmia Marcia, who lived 19 years. Memmius Marcus, her father, and Flavius Martinus, her husband, and Flavius Secundus, her treasurer, to the most pious wife set (the stele).
