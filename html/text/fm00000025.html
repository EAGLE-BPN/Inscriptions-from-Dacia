Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 5-6
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: During the construction of the railway station, Romania
Date: Probably second century, after Ratiaria became colonia civium Romanorum

Bibliography:

  • CIL III 6156
  • ISM II 302

Text

D(is) M(anibus)
Catiliae Respe-
ctae domo Ratia-
ria ob memoriam
5Senproniae (sic!) Rufinae
filiae eius et Allidi Se-
cundini nepotis eius
Cai Allidi Rufinus et
Rufus
10aviae suae et
matri et fratri dul-
cis[sim]is per Allidium Ru-
fum p[atre]m suum faciendum curav-
[eru]nt.
