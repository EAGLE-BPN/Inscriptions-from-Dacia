Translation:

To Jupiter the Good and the Greatest. For the good health of the Emperor Lucius Septumius Severus Pertinax, Flavius Ianuarius, magister of the vicus Clementianensis, set on his own expenses when Dextrus, for the second time, and Trasea Priscus were consuls.
