Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Statue base
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters: 3.6
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: Techirghiol, Romania
Date: Second century AD, before the Severan period, when the Hexapolis transformed into a Pentapolis. Probably the joint reign of Marcus Aurelius and Lucius Verus, or Marcus Aurelius and Commodus, since two emperors are named on the inscription.

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 6, 1882, p. 22, no. 44
  • IGRR I 632
  • ISM II 69

καὶ ἀρχιερέα τῶν β(δουῖν) αὐτοκρατόρων.
