Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Altar
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters: 3.5
Provenance: Oescus
Findspot: ?, Bulgaria
Date: Second-third century AD. After 106 when Oescus became colonia and probably before the middle of the third century.

Bibliography:

  • CIL III 8043
  • ILBulg 23

Text

I(ovi) O(ptimo) M(aximo)
M(arcus) Disius G(eminus?)
aug(ustalis) col(oniae)
Vlp(iae) Oic[i]
5botun p(osuit)

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Commentary:

Connected somehow with M. Disius Iulianus, duumviralis coloniae, ILBulg 44.
