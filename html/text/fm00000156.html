Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 6-8
Provenance: ?
Findspot: in the courtyard of a villager from Slava Rusa, Romania
Date: Second century AD

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, Fouilles, p. 298
  • CIL III 14444
  • Em. Doruțiu-Boilă, SCIV 15, 1964, 1, p. 132, no. 6
  • ISM V 226.

Text

[D(is)] M(anibus)
Tib(erius) Cl(audius) Vet-
tius vix(it) an(nis)
LXXXX Tiberi(i)
5Cl(audii) Ingenus
et Marcus
et Florus.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the Manes Gods! Tiberius Claudius Vettius, who lived 90 years. Tiberii Claudii Ingenus, Marcus and Florus (raised the monument).
