[ - - - - - - - - - - ]perpetuam famam
gloriae semper pro[sequi-]
tur nurus ; pietate nati fre-
quentant ; vixisti grate ;
conv[i]vio celebrantur amici aeternumq(ue) vale nobis pi-

Translation:

....the daughter in law dedicated for the forever living fame; the children see the tomb with piety; you lived with grace; friends are celebrated through a banquet; goodbye forever; the most pious mother...
