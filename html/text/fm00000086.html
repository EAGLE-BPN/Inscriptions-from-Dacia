Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Probably an architrave
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Dedication
Letters: 1.5
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: ?, Romania
Date: Severan period, after the shape of the letters

Bibliography:

  • V. Pârvan, Une nouvelle inscription de Tomi, Dacia 1, 1924, p. 273-279
  • I. Stoian, Tomitana, p. 62-63
  • ISM II 123

Text

[Ἀγαθ]ῆι τύχηι.
Ὁ προστάτης καὶ δισφύλαρχος καὶ φιλότειμος καὶ ἐπιμεληθεὶς τοῦ οἴκου, Ἀπατούριος
Εὐελπίστου τοῦ Ποσειδωνίου τοῦ προστάτου καὶ ἀγαθῶν εὐεργέτη (sic !), ἀνέστησεν φιλοτειμίαν θεοὺς ἐπηκόους.
Φυλὴ Ὁπλείτων ὑπὲρ δισφυλαρχίας.


File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Commentary:

It was published as a plate, although it is clear that it was either an architrave or a part of the entablement, as it was argued by V. Pârvan, p. 273, who also thought that it was plate: "Plaque en pierre calcaire ..., ayant fait partie plutôt d'un entablement que de la base d'un édifice votif".
