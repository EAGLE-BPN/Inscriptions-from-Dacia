Text

Φλάβιον Παννόνιον τὸ[ν]
κράτιστον Αὐρ(ήλιος) Θεόδ[ω-]
ρος Θεοδώρου τὸν [ἐ-]
αυτοῦ εὐεργέτ[ην]
5τειμῆς χάρι[ν]
δ(όγματι) β(ουλῆς) δ(ήμου)

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

In the honor of the most distinguished (equestrian) Flavius Pannonius, Aurelius Teodoros, the son of Teodoros, to his benefactor, honoris causa, the decision reached by the Council and the People.


Commentary:

The monument came most probably from Tomis, being transported in the area, where it was discovered, in the late Roman period.
