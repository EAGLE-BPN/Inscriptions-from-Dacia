Ἀσκληπιάδης Μη-

Translation:

Asklepiades, the son of Menophilos, from Nicomedia and from Aizanoi, trademan, dedicated the altar to the memory of his brother Menophilos the son of Bassus, also trademan; and Asklepiades, the son of Menophilos, dedicated the altar to his father, who lived sixty years. Fare-thee-well, passer-by.
