Translation:

Copied from the minutes posted at statio Resculum from Alburnus Maior: Artemidorus son of Apollonius, magister of the association dedicated to Iupiter Cernenus and Valerius son of Nicos and Offas son of Menofilus, questors of the same association, declare: in the said association, from the 54 initial members, only 17 are still left in Alburnus Maior and Iulius son of Iulius, the other magister, hasn't been to Alburnus since the day of his election. Artemidorus himself cleared his accounts regarding the received money and the money spent for funerals and he also received back the money he had given as guarantee. Now the asociatio has no place for funerals, no money and nobody wants to gather in the established days. Thus, they declare taht the association exists no more and if any of the members was to die, no support will be given for the funeral. Posted in Alburnus Maior, on the 9th of February, when emperor Lucius Aurelius Verus for the third time and Quadratus were consuls.
