Text

Vero III et Quadrato co(n) s(ulibus) IIII kal(endas) Iunias
x quinquaginta L commendatos Lupus Ca
rentis dixit se accepisse et accepit a Iulio
[Al]exandro, quos si redder deb[e]t
5sine ulla controversia.
Actum Albur[no]maiori[[---] ]

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

When Verus for the thirs time and Quadratus were consuls, on the 29th of May, Lupus son of C... received 50 denarii from Iulius Alexander, money that he has to give back..... Sealed at Alburnus Maior...
