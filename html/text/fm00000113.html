Text

Fl(avius) Victor b(ene)f(iciarius) sem(estris)
stip(endiorum) XXIIII vivus sibi f(ecit)
et Flaviae Viviae f(iliae) et Iul(iae)
Longinae coniugi et Flaviis
5V[er]o et Valerio et Vitali f(iliis) b(ene) m(erentis)

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

Flavius Victor, beneficiarius semestris, having served 24 years in the army, being alive made it for him and for Flavia Vivia, his daughter, Iulia Longina, his wife, and for his sons Flavii Verus, Valerius and Vitalis, all being worth to be remember.
