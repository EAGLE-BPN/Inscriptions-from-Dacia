Text

Dis Manibus
Cocceius Vitales vixsit (sic!)
annis L et Coc(ceia) Iulia co-
niunxs (sic!) eius vixsit (sic!) annis
5XL obiti ad villam suam (sic!) Coc-
ceius Clemens et Coc(ceius) He-
lius fili patri et matri [be-]
nemerentibus titulum
posuerunt.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

To the Manes Gods! Cocceius Vitales, who lived 50 years and Cocceia Iulia, his wife, who lived 40 years. They both died at their countryside estate. Cocceius Clemens and Cocceius Helius, their sons, raised the inscription to their well-deserving parents.


Commentary:

For Cocceius Helius see also ISM V 29.
