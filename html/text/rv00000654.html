Text

C(aius) ∙ Arrius Quinṭianus bis-duumviralis et augur muṇ(icipii)Troesmens(is) ∙ vet(erani) ∙ filius ∙ et Clau-dia Servata uxor se vivi[s] me-moriam sibi fecerunt curan-tibus Arriis Qu[i]ntiano filio et Ia-nuario lib[er]to.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

C. Arrius Quintianus, he was duumvir twice and augur of the municipium Troesmis, son of a veteran, and Claudia Servata, his wife, while still alive, he made the funerary monument through the care of Arrius Quintianus, their son, and of (Arrius) Ianuarius, their freedman.
