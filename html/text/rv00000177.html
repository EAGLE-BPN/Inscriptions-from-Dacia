Text

D(is) M(anibus)
P(ublio) Aelio Traiano(?)
vix(it) an(nis) XXVIII
fil(io) et Ael(io) Libero
5alumno v(ixit) a(nnis) III
P(ublius) Ael(ius) Datianus(?)
dec(urio) et aedil(is) col(oniae)
f(aciendum) c(uravit).

Translation:

To the gods Manes of Publius Aelius Traianus, son, who lived 28 years and AeliusLiber, pupil, who lived 3 years. Publius Aelius Dacianus, decurion and edil of the colonia, erected the monument.
