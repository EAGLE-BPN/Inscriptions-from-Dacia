Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Stele
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters:
Provenance: Troesmis
Findspot: Iglita, Romania
Date: The first half of the second century, when V Macedonica legion is attested at Troesmis

Bibliography:

  • Gr. Tocilescu, AEM 6, 1882, p. 45, no. 91
  • CIL III 7503
  • ISM V 192

Text

D(is) M(anibus)
Publiciae Cy-
rillae domo
Bithyna vixit
5annis L C(aius) Pu[bli-]
cius Niger [mil(es)]
leg(ionis) V Ma[c(edonicae) li-]
bertae et co[niu-]
gi b(ene)m(erenti) p[os(uit)].
