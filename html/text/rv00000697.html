Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI

Description


Type of Inscription: funerary
Letters:
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: , Romania
Date: 2nd/3rd C AD

Bibliography:

  • ISM II 298

Text

Ἄττιος Ὀνήσιμος εὐποσιάρχης τὸ μνη-μεῖον κατεσκεύασεν ἐκ τῶν ἰδίων ἑαυ-τῷ ❦ καὶ τοῖς ἰδίοις ❦ ἐὰν δέ τις ἕτερος τολμήσῃτινὰ θε̣ῖ̣ν̣αι ἐν αὐτῷ ❦ δώσει προστείμου ἰς τὸ ταμεῖ-ον μυρία πεντακισχίλια καὶ τῇ πόλει πεντακισχίλια.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

Apios Onesimos, the euposiarch, built the monument, on his own expense, for him and for his (relatives). And if, somebody will dare to put in it somone else, will give, besides the legal punishment, to the (Imperial) treasury 15000 (dinars?), and to the city 5000 (dinars?).
