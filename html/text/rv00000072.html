Translation:

...participant at the Oriental expedition, under Claudius Fronto, Roman senator ?, military tribune of the 15th Apollinaris legion, detached during the first German expedition ?, decurion of the colonia Sarmizegetusa, duumvir, chosen prefect in the place of emperor Commodus....
