Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Florian Matei-Popescu

Description

Object type: Altar
Material: Limestone
Type of Inscription: Funerary
Letters: 6.57.5
Provenance: Tomis
Findspot: In the so-called Trajan's wall, Romania
Date: The first decades of the second century, after the names of the persons mentioned within the text of the inscription.

Bibliography:

  • ISM II 178
