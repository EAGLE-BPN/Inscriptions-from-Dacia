Translation:

To the Emperor Titus Aelius Hadrianus Antoninus Pius, father of the fatherland, holding the power of a tribune and being consul for the third time, and to Verus Caesar, the Roman citizens settled in the canabae of the V Macedonica legion, under (Lucius) Minicius Natalis, the governor of the province, through the care of Cominius Secundus, the commander of the legion.
