Text

C(aius) Iul(ius) C(ai) f(ilius) Quadr[at(us)]
memoriam si-
bi et Iuliae Terentiae con-
iugi suae se vi-
5vo per liberos
suos fecit lo-
ci princeps
quinquenn-
alis territo[rii]
10Capidavensis.

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

See this file in GIT.



Translation:

Caius Iulius, the son of Caius, Quadratus, raised this inscription for him and for Iulia Terentia, his wife, while he was alive through the care of his children. He was the leader of this place and magistrate with censorial powers of the territory of Capidava.
