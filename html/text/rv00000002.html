Source: UNIVERSITATEA BABES BOLYAI


Author: Rada Varga

Description

Object type: plaque
Material: marble
Type of Inscription: building inscription
Execution Technique: carved
Letters: 7-9
Provenance: Ulpia Traiana Sarmizegetusa
Findspot: forum vetus, Romania
Date: 106-117, during Trajan's reign; the first years after 106 are improbable

Bibliography:

  • I. Piso, Le vorum vetus de Sarmizegetusa, Bucuresti, 2006, p. 214-217

Text

Auspiciis
[Imp(eratoris)] Caes(aris) Diui Neruae f[il(ii)]
[Neruae] Traiani Augusti
[Germ(anici) Dac(ici)] condita colonia
5[Ulpia Traiana Augusta] Dacica
[Sarmizegetusa] per
[D(ecimum) Terenti]um Scaurianum
[legatum]eius pro pr(aetore)
[---].

File sorgente prodotto da: EAGLE - Europeana Network of Ancient Greek and Latin Epigraphy

Translation:

The colonia Ulpia Traiana Augusta Dacica Sarmizegetusa, founded under the auspices of the emperor Caesar Nerva Trajan, son of the divine Nerva, Augustus, Germanicus, Dacicus, through Decimus Terentius Scaurianus, his letaus pro praetore...
